About

Last vice chancellor of England. Judge. Eldest son of a barrister and eminent conveyancer and his wife Elizabeth Whitmore. Educated at Eton college, St Johns College, Cambridge (1796). 1800 7th wrangler. Admitted to Lincoln's Inn 1797, called to bar 10 Feb 1803
